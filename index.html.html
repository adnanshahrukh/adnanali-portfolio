How the work moves

One client conversation, six handoffs, one release.

Every feature that reaches a customer passes through the same route — client insight in, working software out. This is the path a request actually takes across OnceHub's client and product sides.

01

Discovery

Runs discovery meetings across multiple lines of business to surface what clients actually need, not just what they ask for.

02

User stories

Converts client pain points into user stories and use cases that feed directly into Product's PRDs.

03

PRD review

Reviews PRDs before sign-off, flagging scope gaps and adoption risk from the client-facing side.

04

Sprint voice

Sits in Engineering's sprint planning, weekly to twice monthly, as the client-side voice on priority.

05

UAT

Tests every release before clients see it, catching shortcomings and routing fixes back to the team.

06

Delivery

Owns the engagement to on-time completion, or keeps it running clean, within a fixed timeline.
